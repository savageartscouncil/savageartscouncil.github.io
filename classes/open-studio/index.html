Open Studio

Open Studio & Gallery
Now Open on Wednesdays

Open Studio is a free weekly event that is a great opportunity to spend time working on your art projects while in the company of other local artists! It is held each Wednesday from 2-6pm at the Savage Arts and Cultural Center, which is located through the east entrance of the Savage Public Library.

If you would like to be on the mailing list for Open Studio, please send an email to Susan at OpenStudio@SavageArtsCouncil.org. The mailing list will be used for reminders, as well as weather cancellations or any other updates. 

Beginning to advanced artists are welcome!

A donation to the Savage Arts Council by becoming a member to help us run this and other programs is appreciated, but optional.
