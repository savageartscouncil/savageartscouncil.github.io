Savage Art Wall Program

The Savage Art Wall program is a collaborative effort between the Savage Arts Council and the City of Savage to bring original artwork displays to Savage residents. Each month a new art exhibit featuring works from area artists or students at local schools will be displayed on the City’s Art Wall, which is located in the main lobby of Savage City Hall, 6000 McColl Drive, Savage MN 55378.

“The City of Savage is pleased to work with the Savage Arts Council help coordinate the art exhibits at Savage City Hall, and introduce local artists and original artwork to our residents.”
– Mayor Janet Williams.

The exhibits are open during normal city hall hours and free to the public. Residents interested in sharing their fine artwork with others are encouraged to email us.
