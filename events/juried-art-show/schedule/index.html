Juried Art Show - Schedule

General Schedule of Events

  • Online Applications Accepted March 13th, 2016 to May 21st, 2016
  • Artists Notified of Acceptance: May 28th, 2016 via email
  • Drop off of All Accepted Entries: June 2nd and 3rd, 2016 3 pm – 7 pm at Cherrywood Pointe Senior Living Facility in Savage
  • Awards & Reception, Cherrywood Pointe senior living community in Savage: Sunday, June 5th, 2:00-3:00 pm (Cherrywood Pointe is located just behind the Savage Public Library in Savage) 5950 W. 130th Lane, Savage, MN 55378, 952.808.8725
  • Show opens to the public at the display venues on Wednesday, June 8th, 2016. Artwork will be on display at the following community locations: Savage Public Library, Savage City Hall, Cub Foods, Klein Bank, Dunn Bros and Cal’s Market
  • Artwork Pick Up Savage Arts and Cultural Center, Savage Public Library, Savage: July 7th & 8th, 2016 3 – 7 pm*

*Savage Arts Council and Cherrywood Pointe will not be responsible for the safety of artwork not picked up. A late fee of $10 per day will be charged for each artwork not picked up during the designated time.
