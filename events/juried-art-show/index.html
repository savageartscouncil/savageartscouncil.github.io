Juried Art Show

The Tenth Annual Juried Art Competition & Show

The Savage Arts Council presented the Tenth Annual Savage Juried Art Competition and Show on June 5th, 2016.

All professional and amateur artists in Minnesota were invited to apply to compete and exhibit their works. The Savage Juried Art Competition and Show is a celebration of visual arts created in Minnesota. This unique community-based show, where the artwork is on display in local businesses and city offices, is open to the public June 8th – July 6th, 2016. Artists across the state come together to showcase the wide variety and depth of art created throughout Minnesota. $1500 in prizes was awarded in four unique categories. Check out our 2016 winners here!
