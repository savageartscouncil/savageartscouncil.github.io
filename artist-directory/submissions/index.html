Artist Directory Submissions

Directory submissions are accepted year-round, and participation in the Directory is free of charge for Savage Art Council members. If you are not a member and would like to join, please hop on over to our Membership page!

Participation in the Artists Directory provides the following benefits:

  1. Promotion for the artist.
  2. The opportunity to be featured as the “Artist of the Month” on the Savage Arts Council Facebook page.
  3. The ability for artists to showcase an image of their work in a unique online gallery. Artists can include their contact information, including website and/or email, in order for art enthusiasts to reach the artist with inquiries. 
  4. Networking with other artists – it’s fun to see who else is out there, when creating can be such a solitary process!

If you would like to be listed in the Artist Directory, please email Tracey with the following information to include in your listing:

  • Name
  • Medium(s)
  • Email
  • Website
  • An image of your work, no smaller than 600x600 pixels
