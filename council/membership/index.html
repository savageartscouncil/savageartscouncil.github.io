Membership

Become a Member or Give the Gift of Membership

Savage Arts Council founded in 2006, strives to provide leadership in arts awareness, education, programming and artist opportunities within Savage and surrounding communities. Savage Arts Council is a registered 501(c)(3) and works to support the arts at all levels within our community and region with programs and events. Savage Arts Council Membership helps support the largest non-profit fine art organization in Scott County Minnesota. With your membership we can continue to provide free and low cost events and activities and support local artist opportunities:

  • Art events like the Savage Juried Art Show and the Scott County Art Crawl.
  • Fine art exhibits like the City Art Wall, Juried Art Show and the gallery walls at the Savage Arts & Cultural Center.
  • Cultural art events close to home.
