Board of Directors

Board of Directors

The Savage Arts Council holds monthly meetings that all members are welcome to attend. They are held the second Monday of each month from 6 to 8pm at the Savage Arts and Cultural Center, which is located through the east entrance of the Savage Public library at 13090 Alabama Ave S, Savage, MN 55378.

Any board member can be reached by sending an email to info@SavageArtsCouncil.org
